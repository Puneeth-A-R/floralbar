About us

Floral Bar is committed to the organic flower movement and reducing the footprint within the floral and landscaping industry. We have partnered with various organic flower farmers in Michigan. We also only use organic practices in the gardens that we install.

A lot goes into creating a garden from conception to construction. The Team at Floral Bar works enthusiastically with each other and our subcontractors. We are dedicated to creating stunning outside spaces to the tangible reality for our clients.
